01

What is the purpose of this E.coli DNA Analysis system?

This system uses Artificial Intelligence to analyze E.coli DNA sequences and predict whether a given gene is associated with a disease or not. It helps researchers and students quickly identify patterns without manual analysis.

02

What type of file should I upload for analysis?

You need to upload a DNA sequence file in .fna (FASTA nucleotide) format. This format is commonly available from biological databases like NCBI.

03

How does the prediction model work?

The system extracts trimer (3-letter nucleotide) frequencies from the DNA sequence and feeds them into a trained machine learning model. Based on learned patterns, the model predicts whether the gene is classified as Disease or Normal.

04

Can I view and manage my previous predictions?

Yes, all your predictions are stored securely. You can view them in the My Data section, edit gene names, or delete records anytime. This helps in tracking and managing your analysis history.

05

Is the prediction result 100% accurate?

No prediction model is 100% accurate. The results are based on trained data and statistical patterns. This system is intended for educational and research purposes, and results should be validated with biological experiments for critical applications.
