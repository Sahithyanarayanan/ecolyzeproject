About

Our AI-based platform analyzes gene sequences of E.coli using trimer counts to predict pathogenic potential. Explore sample sequences, predictive analytics, and insights for research and bioinformatics applications.

AI-Powered Disease Prediction

Our platform predicts pathogenic potential of E.coli strains using trimer count analysis and machine learning models trained on genomic data. We provide accurate, interpretable, and fast predictions to support researchers and bioinformatics professionals.

The system allows users to upload DNA sequences, compare them with reference genomes, visualize trimer distributions, and obtain predictive results for better decision-making in microbiology and genomics research.
